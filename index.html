Meet the Owner
Dr. Rizwan

Dr. Rizwan founded R1 Fitness Gym on a simple belief: that a world-class training environment should be accessible to every member of the Satellite Town community. His hands-on leadership and genuine passion for fitness shape every corner of both branches — from the equipment on the floor to the culture among the trainers.

Known by members for his humility and approachability, Dr. Rizwan is dedicated to the growth of every person who walks through the door, whether they're chasing their first pull-up or preparing for a competition stage. His commitment to a clean, welcoming, and motivating space is what keeps members coming back, day after day.
