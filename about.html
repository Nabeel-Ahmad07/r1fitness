Our Story

More than a gym — a community built on discipline, humility, and real transformation in the heart of Satellite Town, Rawalpindi.

How It Started

The R1 Fitness Story

R1 Fitness Gym was built on a simple idea: Rawalpindi deserved a training space that matched the standards of the world's best gyms, without losing the warmth of a neighborhood community. What began as a single floor in Satellite Town has grown into two thriving branches, each shaped by the same founding values.

Under the leadership of Dr. Rizwan, the gym — formerly known as Oxygen Gym on Main Saidpur Road — was reimagined with modern equipment, certified coaching, and a culture that treats every member like family. Today, R1 Fitness Gym stands as one of the area's most trusted names in strength and transformation.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Philosophy

Our Mission

To provide every member of our community with access to premium training, expert guidance, and a space that makes real transformation possible, 24 hours a day.

Our Vision

To become Rawalpindi's most trusted fitness brand — known for genuine results, a clean and welcoming environment, and a coaching team that truly cares.

Our Philosophy

Discipline over motivation, consistency over intensity, and community over competition. Strength is built one honest rep at a time.

Meet The Founder

Dr. Rizwan

Dr. Rizwan leads R1 Fitness Gym with a hands-on approach rarely found in the industry. He's on the floor, he knows the members by name, and he treats every question — from a first-timer or a competitive lifter — with the same patience and respect.

His dedication to keeping both branches clean, well-equipped, and genuinely welcoming has earned R1 Fitness Gym a reputation built on trust rather than marketing. For Dr. Rizwan, success is measured by the members who show up, week after week, stronger than before.
